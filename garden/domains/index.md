# Domains

Domains are broad research areas that organize clusters of ideas, projects, publications, and essays.

## Main domains

- [[attention-and-meditation]] gathers work on attentional training, mindfulness, meditation, thought suppression, perception, creativity, and self-regulation.
- [[dreams-and-low-control-states]] gathers work on dreams, automaticity, self-control, and situations where deliberate regulation is reduced.
- [[polarization-and-prejudice]] gathers work on affective polarization, prejudice regulation, common identity, implicit bias, and democratic participation.
- [[self-identity-and-agency]] gathers work on self-identification, agency, motivation, autonomy, belief, internalization, and personality.
- [[social-change-and-prosociality]] gathers work on prosocial behavior, moral habits, identity expansion, wise interventions, social norms, and policy-relevant behavior change.
